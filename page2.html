2. Directions & Transportation

By Air (from Sabah, Sarawak, Kedah, Perlis, etc.)

Most attendees flying in will land at Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KUL). From there, you can reach the stadium:

  • Train (Fastest public option): Take the KLIA Transit train from KUL to Bandar Tasik Selatan (~40 min). From Bandar Tasik Selatan, transfer to the RapidKL Sri Petaling LRT line toward Bukit Jalil LRT Station (~7 min). Walk ~5–10 minutes to the entrance.
  • Taxi / Ride-Share: Direct ride from KUL to Bukit Jalil takes ~40 min by car/taxi/ride-share (Grab).
  • Bus (Budget): Shuttle buses run from KLIA to Terminal Bersepadu Selatan (TBS); then use LRT or local buses.

By Public Transport (within Klang Valley)

  • LRT (Recommended): RapidKL Sri Petaling Line services Bukit Jalil LRT Station — just a short (~5–10 min) walk from the stadium.
  • From KL Sentral: Take the LRT line via Masjid Jamek or transfer routes — total ~25–30 min ride.
  • Bus Services: Several RapidKL buses such as T563, T580, 652 stop near the Bukit Jalil area.

By Car / Taxi

Drive via Maju Expressway (MEX) and local roads toward Bukit Jalil National Stadium. The stadium is well-signposted. Parking is available but fills up early on event days.

Quick Transit Notes

From the airport, the train + LRT combination is often faster and more reliable than road transport during peak hours. Public transport often extends service hours during major events.
